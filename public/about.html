About Retriever

One-stop-shop for Canine Resources

Retreiver allows dogs - and their owners - to search for local dog-friendly restaurants, dog parks and beaches, dog washes and groomers, and dog trainers.

Connect with Others

Retreiver allows users to create profiles, search for fur friends by various criteria (breed, age, favoriate activities, location, and more), and connect with a large user community for information on events, recommendations, and any other dog-related topic.

Dog-Experience Focused

Retreiver presents the world through the eyes of your canine best friend. In this world, humans are the side kicks!
